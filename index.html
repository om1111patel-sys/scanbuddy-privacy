Privacy Policy for ScanBuddy

ScanBuddy is an inventory tracking application designed for scanning North Carolina scratch-off ticket barcodes.

Information Collection

The ScanBuddy app does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Camera Usage

The app uses the device camera solely to scan barcodes from lottery tickets. No images or video are stored or transmitted.

Data Storage

All scanned data remains on the device and is only used to generate PDF reports that the user may choose to share.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking services.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the developer.

This app is not affiliated with or endorsed by the North Carolina Education Lottery.
